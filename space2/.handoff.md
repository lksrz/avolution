## Signal Model (v17.1 — continuous, no dead zones)
12 signals, ALL continuous (no thresholds that create dead zones):

| Signal | Weight/Cap | Description |
|--------|-----------|-------------|
| **rev** | 0.12 | Reversal of last candle direction (~67% alternation rate) |
| **t5** | cap 0.12 | 5-candle price trend (proportional) |
| **t10** | cap 0.10 | 10-candle price trend (proportional) |
| **mr_dn/mr_up** | 0.30 | Mean reversion when 4+ same-direction streak |
| **mom** | 0.10w | 3-candle direction momentum |
| **sma** | 40x, cap 0.20 | Price vs 10-SMA mean reversion |
| **rsi** | 0.30w | RSI centered at 50 (continuous) |
| **ema** | 40x, cap 0.15 | EMA 5/12 crossover (continuous) |
| **bb** | 0.30w | Bollinger Bands position mean reversion |
| **cur** | cap 0.10 | Current incomplete candle momentum |
| **alt** | 0.10 | Strict 4-candle alternation pattern |
| **vol** | 0.08 | High volume + strong body = continuation |

## What Works
1. **Continuous signals** — NO thresholds. Every signal contributes proportionally. This was the key breakthrough (v16 had dead zones that made the model always predict UP).
2. **Reversal signal** — BTC 5m candles alternate direction ~67% of the time. Simple bet-against-last-candle is our strongest single signal.
3. **Mean reversion on streaks** — When 4+ candles go the same direction, betting on reversal works.
4. **verify_all_pending()** — Scans ALL PENDING log entries against candle data, not just the last state. Critical fix from Loop 3.

## What Doesn't Work / Lessons Learned
1. **NEVER use accuracy feedback** — v14 had signals that adjusted based on past accuracy. Created a death spiral: wrong predictions → adjusted wrong → more wrong. Accuracy crashed to 16.7%.
2. **Threshold-based signals** — v16 had thresholds (e.g., "if RSI > 70 then -0.3, else 0"). Most signals fell in dead zones and produced 0. Model defaulted to weak momentum → always predicted UP.
3. **Single signal dominance** — t5 was originally capped at 0.20 which could override all other signals. Reduced to 0.12.
4. **Trend-following in choppy markets** — Our two wrong predictions (21:40, 22:00) both predicted UP during downtrends. The model's mean-reversion and trend signals can conflict.
