## Signal Model (v17.1 — continuous, no dead zones)
12 signals, ALL continuous (no thresholds):

| Signal | Weight/Cap | Description |
|--------|-----------|-------------|
| **rev** | 0.12 | Reversal of last candle (~67% alternation rate — best single edge) |
| **t5** | cap 0.12 | 5-candle price trend |
| **t10** | cap 0.10 | 10-candle price trend |
| **mr_dn/mr_up** | 0.15-0.30 | Mean reversion on 4+ streaks (reduced to 0.15 when trend confirms streak) |
| **mom** | 0.10w | 3-candle direction momentum |
| **sma** | 40x | Price vs 10-SMA mean reversion |
| **rsi** | 0.30w | RSI centered at 50 |
| **ema** | 40x | EMA 5/12 crossover |
| **bb** | 0.30w | Bollinger Bands mean reversion |
| **cur** | cap 0.10 | Current incomplete candle momentum |
| **alt** | 0.10 | Strict 4-candle alternation pattern |
| **vol** | 0.08 | High volume + strong body = continuation |

## What Works
1. **Continuous signals** — NO thresholds. Every signal contributes proportionally.
2. **Reversal signal** — BTC 5m candles alternate ~67%. Simple bet-against-last-candle is strongest edge.
3. **Mean reversion on streaks** — 4+ same direction usually reverses.
4. **verify_all_pending()** — Scans ALL PENDING log entries, not just last state.
5. **Trend-dampened mean reversion** — mr_dn reduced from 0.30 to 0.15 when both t5 and t10 confirm the downtrend.

## What Doesn't Work / Lessons Learned
1. **NEVER use accuracy feedback** — v14 death spiral (16.7% accuracy).
2. **Threshold-based signals** — v16 dead zones made model always predict UP.
3. **Single signal dominance** — t5 capped from 0.20→0.12.
4. **Mean reversion vs persistent trends** — 3 of our 3 wrong predictions were UP during downtrends. The mr_dn fix (Loop 33) reduces this but doesn't eliminate it.

## Ideas for Improvement
1. **Weight reversal signal higher** (0.15?) — most reliable edge
2. **Add 1-minute candle analysis** for finer-grained near-deadline updates
3. **Further reduce mr_dn** when trend is strong — or make it proportional to streak length
4. **Time-of-day patterns** — BTC behavior differs across sessions
